Our Yoga Training Course

What You Will Learn

Yoga Philosophy: Introduction, aim, meaning, and yogic practices.

  • Origin, history, and development of yoga.
  • Yogic etymology, definition, objectives, and miscommunications.
  • Introduction to Sankhya yoga darshan.
  • Streams of yoga (Jnana, Raja, Bhakti, and Karma yoga).
  • Life and messages by spiritual masters.
  • Three gunas â€“ sattva, rajas, tamas.
  • Maya, prakriti, brahman, avidya.
  • Atman, parmataman, jiva.
  • Four internal components: mind, intellect, subconscious, and ego.
  • Bahiranga and Antaranga yoga of Maharshi Patanjali.
  • Practicing Abhyasa and cultivating Vairagya (detachment) essential for spiritual development.
Yoga Asanas
  • The basic structure of the introductory course.
  • Surya namaskar
  • Deep relaxation / Yoga Nidra
  • Bandhas and mudras
  • Meditation techniques and prayers
  • Concept of Yoga asana, pranayama and kriyas
  • Concept and principle of Sukshma vyayama, sthulavyayama and Surya namaskar.
